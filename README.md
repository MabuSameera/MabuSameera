### 🚲 Bike Sales Analytics Dashboard
**Tools:** Excel · SQL · Python · Power BI

**Problem:** Understand what drove revenue growth and margin trade-offs across product lines and regions.

**Approach:**
- Cleaned & structured **113,036 transactions** (2017–2021, 6 countries) in Python/Pandas
- Aggregated by country, category, and customer segment using SQL & Power Query
- Built a Power BI dashboard with DAX measures for YoY revenue, margin %, and segment share

**Impact:**
- 📈 Quantified **189% revenue growth** (2017→2021): $10.3M → $29.7M
- 🚴 Found Bikes drove **73% of revenue** but only a **40% margin** vs **63%** for Accessories
- 🎯 Just **2 of 17** sub-categories generated **64% of revenue** → supported a margin-focused product mix recommendation

</td>

### 🛒 Online Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard
**Tools:** Excel · SQL · Tableau · Power Bi

**Problem:** Identify revenue concentration and seasonality in UK online retail transactions.

**Approach:**
- Cleaned **530,104 transactions** (Dec 2010–Dec 2011, 4,338 customers, 3,922 products)
- Removed **11,805 cancelled/invalid orders** (2.2%) in Excel/SQL
- Built an interactive Tableau dashboard with Pareto and time-series views

**Impact:**
- 💰 Top **20% of SKUs** generated **79.4% of revenue**
- 👥 Top **20% of customers** drove **74.6% of revenue** → case for a loyalty program
- 📆 Seasonal spike from **£640K (June) → £1.5M (November)** → informed holiday stock planning
